ABOUT META MONACO

Our mission is to generate an Art experience that can impact you in a positive way because Art changes lives

In ancient Greek meta means beyond. In essence the gallery concept is driven towards elevated emotions. The gallery space in Monte-Carlo fuses art and technology. META presents masterpieces from exceptional antiques to contemporary artists of global significance. META hosts regularly events at the gallery in Monte-Carlo and in New York City. Stay tuned to attend META events!
