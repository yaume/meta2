ABOUT META MONACO

"Our mission is to generate an experience that can affect you in a positive way because Art impacts lives"

META is Monaco's 1st concept gallery. A young brand headed by three Malibu, CA raised siblings. Kira, Marina and Roy define the company's very exclusive bohemian style in art, jewelry and design.

META is based in Monaco and also in New York City, where the brand introduces its new jewelry collections.

META's original creations share distinct casual attributes that make them genuinely cosmopolitan. The collectible pieces are always locally handmade by the finest if not most avant garde craftsmanship.

In ancient Greek 'meta' means beyond. In essence the concept tends towards sensorial elevation. The flagship gallery by the beach in Monte-Carlo fuses art, jewelry and design with technology.

META regularly hosts events in Monaco and in New York, so stay tuned!
